How to use this screen sharing system:

  1. Click the "Share Screen" button to start sharing your screen.
  2. Select the screen or application window you want to share.
  3. Your screen will be visible to all participants in the system.
  4. Click "Stop Sharing" when you're done.
  5. To view someone else's shared screen, simply wait for it to appear.
